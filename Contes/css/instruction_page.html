Pattern

- The test consists of multiple choice questions (MCQ type). In this, for each given question, the applicant must choose the right, or the closest, answer from among the choices given.

- The test is a half hours (30 minutes) long.

- It consists of 25 questions carrying 100 marks.

- There is no negative marking.

- Unanswered questions will receive no marks.

Procedure

- Get comfortable with your computer before you begin the online test.

- Check the internet connection properly.

- All questions are multiple choice questions and you are required to select the correct answer from the given choices. Only one of the given responses is correct or the most appropriate. You need to click on the button next to the answer.

- You can navigate and look through through the questions.
